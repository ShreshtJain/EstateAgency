The Jhansi Development Authority (JDA) is the government body that regulates land use, construction, and development in Jhansi. A property with JDA approval ensures that it follows proper zoning, building, and safety norms.

Visit the official JDA website (www.jdaup.in) or office to verify project details. You can request the builder for the JDA approval letter and sanctioned building plan before investing.

RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority) is a government act that regulates the real estate sector. It ensures transparency, timely project delivery, and protects buyers against false promises by developers.

You can search for the project on the Uttar Pradesh RERA website (www.up-rera.in). All new residential and commercial projects with more than 8 units or 500 sq.m. of land must be registered with RERA.

Apart from the property price, buyers usually pay:

  • Stamp duty (7% in UP) + Registration charges (1%)
  • GST (for under-construction properties: 1% for affordable housing, 5% for others)
  • Maintenance charges (for flats)
  • Legal and brokerage fees (if any)
