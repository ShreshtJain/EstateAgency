Eldeco County

Step into a world of modern living at Eldeco County, Jhansi — where thoughtfully designed homes meet lush surroundings, comfort, and convenience.

Overview

Eldeco County is a thoughtfully planned community of 118 independent expandable villas, offering the luxury of wide-open layouts, private gardens, sunlit terraces, and dedicated driveways.

Spread across 32 acres of lush greens, it blends modern living with nature, creating a serene and refreshing environment away from city chaos.

Located on prime land along Jhansi-Lalitpur Road at Rajgarh, Eldeco County is just 6 km from the city center, keeping you close to schools, markets, and hospitals while enjoying a peaceful private community.

Here, you don’t just buy a villa — you invest in a lifestyle of freedom, greenery, and future-ready comfort.
