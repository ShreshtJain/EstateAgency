Pioneering Strength & Style in Jhansi

Impressa Safety Glass is the first toughened glass manufacturing facility in Jhansi and the Bundelkhand region, setting new standards in safety, durability, and design. With advanced technology and precision processes, the factory specializes in producing high-quality toughened and safety glass that meets modern architectural and industrial needs.

In addition to glass manufacturing, Impressa also works in uPVC glass solutions and custom furniture making, bringing together innovation, functionality, and style under one roof.

As a regional leader, Impressa Safety Glass stands for quality, trust, and modern craftsmanship, delivering products that are both strong and elegant.
