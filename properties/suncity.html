Suncity Colony

Experience the perfect blend of nature and modernity in this thoughtfully designed township. With spacious plots and elegant villas set amidst green landscapes, it offers a peaceful yet well-connected lifestyle, just minutes away from the city.

Live Green. Live Modern. Live Connected.

Spread across 20 acres of thoughtfully planned land, this township marks a milestone in the city’s journey towards modern community living. Wide roads, landscaped areas, and open green spaces shape an environment that balances comfort with convenience.

Within the township are plots and villas of various sizes, offering diverse living options while maintaining a cohesive neighborhood character. The design emphasizes openness, greenery, and a sense of community that grows stronger with time.

Located just 7 km from Elite Crossing on Unnao–Balaji Road, the township enjoys easy connectivity to the heart of the city while maintaining a peaceful residential setting.
